To My Dearest Mentor, Guide, and Mother,
Mam Saima Umer

Dearest Mom,
In English we simply say "Mom",
But to me, You are much more -
A tutor ,A Friend, A guiding light,
The wisdom that helps my dreams take flight.

You understand me when words fall short,
Lift me up with endless support.
When I stumble, You make me strong,
Turning doubts into hope lifelong.
You don’t just teach; you inspire my soul,
Filling my journey, making me whole.
A protector, a beacon, forever so kind,
A mother, a teacher, my greatest find.

The universe wrote its finest verse
When it made you my tutor first.
